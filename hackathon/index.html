The Hackathon

It is with great excitement that Oakton CSCS Club in partnership with null.black and Fixotech announces the Oakhacks hackathon. Oakhacks Hackathon will take place on the 18th and 19th of April from 12:00pm to 12:00pm

Registration is open to all Highschool students in the Northern Virginia area and must be done through our registration form. Throughout the hackathon, officers of Oakton CSCS club will be providing technical support and mentoring.

Challenge: Build out a project from an idea to a workable prototype withing the 24 hour time limit with a team of up to 3 people. This project can be software or hardware and written in any language you prefer.

Prize: Each person on the top three teams will recieve a $25 amazon gift card, one free year of null.black vpn, and 100GB of free Fixotech cloud file storage.

Rules

Teams may not have more than 3 people.

All persons wishing to participate must fill out this registration form.

In order for the team to begin working, an open repository must be created on github to contain the code used in the project. If your project is 100% hardware, you should upload photos to this repo.

Winning teams will be subject to a code review after the event, or before receiving their prize(s).

The use of code that is already written is prohibited! Everyone will start the hackathon at the same time. This will ensure impartiality of the competition. Do not participate in the hackathon if you are going to reuse an existing project.
